Expo in Snuffel hostel in BRUGES.
12 January till 12 May.

"This series of photos was made on the Belgian coast, a walk from Knokke to the Panne, in the sand on the water, which did not make it easy. So Kaili had time to stand still and look around her and so she began to noticed serten details. When she looked at the ground, she felt like she was making aerial photographs form miles away in the sky, She actually sees the photos as abstract, because when she looks at the photos she sees more than details, it gives her a feeling that she is a new create a landscape."

Expo Triënale Bruges: Kunstenal-off parcours-

4 May till 16 September .

INSPIRED BY (TRIËNALE): -OFF PARCOURS-, the city of Bruges wants to give young creatives and artists a platform, so that they can participate in the art event, such as the Trienale. At various locations throughout the city, there are works to see made by 10 Bruges artists.

The series of photographs that can be seen in the window of the Belfius Bank (Sint-Salvatorskerkhof 18, in Bruges center), is a series made at various locations in Ghent.
